Lesson Requirements:

  • parent or guardian must be at all in-person lessons
  • piano
    • must be in good working order
    • in many cases I am able to tune and maintain client pianos, see the equipment FAQ
  • method book
    • provided for free digitally
    • client can either print the digital book or use a tablet with notation capability
    • printed book must be double-sided so duets show on facing pages
  • lesson notebook for writing assignments
  • videoconferencing device
    • if using Android or iOS, install Jitsi Meet app
    • if using MacOS, Windows, or Linux, go to meet.jit.si using Chrome or Chromium web browser
    • enter the provided meeting name and password
    • occasionally other students are invited to online lessons to provide performance opportunities
    • like other videoconferencing software, Jitsi will have occasional quirks and interruptions, and these are factored into lesson prices
    • I use Jitsi because it is more private and has better audio quality than Zoom or Skype
  • metronome
  • good Internet connection
  • lesson fees paid ahead in 4 or 13 week blocks
    • twenty-six 12-1/2 minute lessons (13 weeks): $130
    • eight 12-1/2 minute lessons (4 weeks): $48
    • lessons on non-school days are optional
    • lesson times can be moved or refunded ONLY in the following cases:
      • students prepaying for 13 weeks may change lesson time once
      • rescheduling allowed if Internet issues starting during a lesson exceed six minutes in total
      • death in the immediate family
      • illness requiring professional care
      • moving more than 20 miles away
      • immobilized finger(s)
  • ukulele (optional)
    • $45 deposit is refundable if returned with only light wear-and-tear
