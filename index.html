Welcome to Syracuse Piano!

My lessons compliment other extracurricular activities, cater to different learning styles, and build responsibility, critical thinking, and artistry.

Lessons are online other than the first lesson of each month, which covers what isn't efficiently taught online. This allows two 12-1/2 minute lessons a week to help students absorb material and pace their practicing better.

You can request a few lesson times after going over what you need to get started.

In addition to lessons, I also tune and maintain musical equipment and produce recordings.
