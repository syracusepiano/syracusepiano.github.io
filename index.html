Welcome to Syracuse Piano! I teach lessons, tune and maintain musical equipment, and produce recordings.

Lessons are online other than the first lesson of each month, which covers what isn't efficiently taught online. This allows two 12-1/2 minute lessons a week to help students absorb material and pace their practicing better.

Lessons are M-F evenings and maintenance Th/F/Sat afternoons. Enter several scheduling options into this form to request lessons or maintenance.
